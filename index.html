The mountain

Ninety-seven acres on top of a mountain

An old colliery tip reclaimed into five tracks, sat on a plateau above the Heads of the Valleys with the whole of South Wales laid out below.

Before you ride

Common questions

What kit do I need?
Helmet, goggles, boots, gloves and full-length riding kit are required on every track, no exceptions. Body armour and neck protection are strongly recommended.
Which track should I start on?
Start one level below where you think you belong. Marshals can move you up once they've seen you ride — it's the fastest and safest way to find your level.
Do I need a licence to race?
Race events require a valid NORA92 licence. If you don't hold an annual one, a £15 one-event day licence covers your insurance and eligibility for a single event.
Can I spectate on event days?
Yes — spectators are welcome at races. Keep to marked zones, children supervised at all times, and follow marshal directions. Racing runs in most weather.
